What i have worked on over the years

Johnny's Flight

Leap into the daring escapade of Johnny, the intrepid frog seeking freedom from the confines of the zoo. Imprisoned in a cage, Johnny embarks on a perilous journey through the intricate sewer system beneath the zoo. Navigate treacherous waters, solve clever puzzles, and overcome various challenges as Johnny hops towards liberty. Along the way, encounter quirky sewer denizens, unlock abilities, and unveil the secrets of the labyrinthine passages.

Whiskers the cat

Embark on a heartwarming journey with "Whiskers the cat: A Forest Tale" a delightful game where an orange cat named Whiskers befriends a lost little girl in the enchanted forest. Guide them through captivating landscapes, solving puzzles, and overcoming challenges on their way back to the girl's worried parents. Along the way, form a bond that transcends the ordinary, as Whiskers helps the girl find not only her family but also a family of her own within the magical depths of the woods.

Behind

In the neon-lit alleyways and towering skyscrapers of Neo-Purr City, a tiny rebel emerges from the shadows. Meet Squeaks, an ordinary mouse with extraordinary resilience, captured by the ruling cat overlords. In "Behind," navigate the treacherous world of whiskers and claws as Squeaks endeavors to stay one step ahead, avoiding capture and uncovering the dark secrets that lurk "Behind."

Hoppington

Embark on an epic journey with Hoppington, a courageous bunny samurai on a mission to rescue his kidnapped family. Armed with a tiny yet razor-sharp katana, guide Hoppington through lush landscapes as he faces formidable wolf adversaries. With swift and strategic combat, players must help our furry hero prevail against the odds, showcasing the indomitable spirit of a bunny samurai determined to save those he holds dear.
